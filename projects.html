Applied Physics Research Paper

Energy Optimization in Renewable Systems

December 2024

Computational Modeling & Optimization

Developed a Python-based stochastic optimization framework to minimize renewable energy shortfalls in solar and wind power systems. Used historical data from 1965-2023 to model realistic supply trends and optimize energy storage allocation.

Key Contributions:
  • Implemented stochastic gradient descent (SGD) with momentum to optimize energy storage allocation
  • Conducted 1,000 Monte Carlo simulations to model uncertainties in supply and demand
  • Calculated advanced risk metrics including Value at Risk (VaR) and 95% confidence intervals
  • Leveraged global dataset from Our World in Data, Ember, and the Energy Institute (1965-2023)
  • Visualized energy trends, demand variability, and optimized shortfalls for diverse audiences
